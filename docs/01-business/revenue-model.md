# Revenue Model

## Current State (Free Platform)

**Revenue**: $0

**Cost Structure**: Minimal (AI automation, hosting costs only)

**Sustainability**: Funded to reach critical mass of users

## Future Revenue (Educational App - Separate Project)

### Parent Subscriptions
- Monthly/annual subscription to developmental tracking app
- Pricing: TBD
- Value: EYLF-based activities, AI insights, Instagram-style progress feed

### Nanny Commissions
- Nannies earn commission on parent signups they facilitate
- Incentivizes nannies to promote educational app to matched families
- Creates aligned incentives

### Optional Training Bundles
- Parents can purchase nanny training package
- Baby Bloom trains the parent's chosen nanny in developmental best practices
- Premium add-on service

## Expansion Plans

Geographic expansion only after:
- Platform proven in Sydney
- Critical mass of users achieved
- Product-market fit confirmed
- Educational app launched and validated
