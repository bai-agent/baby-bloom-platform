# Competitive Analysis

## Traditional Sydney Nanny Agencies

### Pricing Model:
- $400 finder's fee for 1 day/week placement
- $1,200 finder's fee for 5 days/week placement
- Often take ongoing commission from nanny earnings

### Service Level:
- Manual matchmaking (slow, limited options)
- Basic screening
- No technology-driven matching
- No ongoing support tools

### Pain Points They Create:

**For Nannies:**
- High barrier to entry (fees or commission cuts)
- Limited job opportunities (manual matching = fewer connections)
- No tools to market themselves

**For Parents:**
- Expensive ($400-$1,200 just to get introduced)
- Limited selection (manual curation = smaller pool)
- No transparency in matching criteria
